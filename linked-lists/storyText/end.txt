Breathing heavily, your weapons slick with the blood of his guardians, 
you push deeper into the cave and emerge into a vast, cathedral-like chamber. 
And there it is. Coiled upon a throne of bones and scavenged metal is the King Cobra. 
It is immense, its body as thick as an ancient oak tree and 
stretching the length of a football field. Its scales shimmer with an oily, 
intelligent iridescence, and its eyes, the color of molten gold, 
fix you with a gaze that holds no animal instinct, only pure, malevolent cunning. 
This is the mind that has orchestrated the world's downfall. 
There is no more hesitation, no more choice. 
To save the land, you must face this god of the wasteland and defeat it. Now.
