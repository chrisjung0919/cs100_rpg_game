You finally arrive at the mouth of a massive cave, 
a jagged wound in the side of a mountain range that reeks of decay and reptile musk. 
This is it. Taking a deep breath, you step inside,
 your light piercing the oppressive darkness.
  The air is thick and humid, and the walls gleam with a strange moisture.
   You haven't gone fifty feet before the first pair of glowing eyes appear, 
   then another, and another. The King's lesser servants are here to guard the way—a pack of mutated, 
   wolf-like lizards with too many teeth and a desperate hunger. 
   You must fight through them, a brutal, chaotic melee in the echoing dark, 
   each victory bringing you closer to the final confrontation.
