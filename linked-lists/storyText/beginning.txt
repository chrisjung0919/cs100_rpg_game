                The world as we knew it has crumbled into a chaotic nightmare. 
        It all started when a rogue asteroid, its trajectory horribly miscalculated, 
    passed far too close to Earth, showering the planet in a strange, persistent radioactive dust. 
                 From this fallout, nature itself was twisted and corrupted. 
    Ordinary reptiles swelled into hulking, predatory lizards with scales like armor. 
                    Desert arachnids mutated into giant scorpions, 
        their venomous stingers capable of felling a grown man in seconds. 
    But the worst are the bandits—survivors who lost their humanity and now ride alongside these beasts, 
        scavenging and terrorizing whatever pockets of civilization remain. 
        And they all answer to a single, terrifying intelligence: a colossal, 
    sentient snake known only as the King Cobra, whose dominion over the wasteland is absolute. 
            There is no safe ground left, which is why we've sought you out. 
                Your particular skillset is our last, desperate hope. 
            If you can end the King, we might finally have a chance to rebuild.
