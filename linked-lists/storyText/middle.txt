You make your choice and press onward, 
the weight of the wasteland pressing down on you. 
The land is a scarred and barren hellscape, 
every resource long since scavenged by the relentless creatures.
 Canteens are empty, the few edible plants are nothing but ash, 
 and the oppressive heat warps the horizon into a liquid dance of misery.
  You're parched, your lips cracked, when a true miracle appears: 
  a lush, green oasis, its palm trees a stark contrast to the grey-brown nothingness.
   Hope surges within you as you stumble closer, 
   but it curdles instantly into ice in your veins.
    Scattered around the water's edge are the skeletal remains of other soldiers,
     their gear picked clean and their bones bleached white by the sun. 
     Before you can retreat, 
     a grizzled figure in makeshift armor steps out from behind a rock,
      a wicked grin splitting his dust-caked face. It's a trap, 
      and this bandit has been using the oasis as a lure for wanderers just like you.
